Collab Teams

Collab Teams should be equipped to autonomously resolve blockers, functioning as self-contained entities within the larger organization. With a diverse array of expertise and decision-making authority vested within the team, you'll be empowered to efficiently overcome obstacles and sustain momentum.

“Agile processes promote sustainable development. The sponsors, developers, and users should be able to maintain a constant pace indefinitely.”
— Agile Manifesto

For instance, in cloud-centric businesses, integrating a DevOps engineer directly into the team is imperative. Similarly, for companies prioritizing design aesthetics, including a UX designer or engineer is crucial. Likewise, organizations reliant on complex data models may benefit from incorporating a dedicated DBA into their focused team. This approach eliminates the need for distinct “DevOps teams,” “UX teams,” or “Data teams,” instead facilitating collaboration across all facets of the company in cohesive “vertical slices.”
